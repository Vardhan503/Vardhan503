<h1 align="center">Hi, I'm Sai Vardhan Reddy Narla 👋</h1>

I build AI systems that hold up in production — and I got here through the backend.

I spent 2.5 years as a software engineer at **Zomato**, deep in the systems that keep a food-delivery platform running at scale: Kafka pipelines, Redis caching, Elasticsearch search, and the on-call shifts that teach you what actually breaks under load. Then I came to the U.S. for my **MS in Computer Science at George Washington University** to go deep on applied AI.

Now I work on **LLM and RAG systems** — retrieval pipelines, evaluation tooling, and the agentic logic that keeps model responses fast, grounded, and honest about what they don't know.

- 🚀 Previously built RAG ingestion and evaluation infrastructure for **Chat Concierge** (Capital One's first multi-agent AI assistant).
- ⚡ Scaled distributed backend infrastructure to sustain **8,400+ peak orders per minute** at **Zomato**.
- 💡 I care about **latency, graceful fallbacks, and observability** — the unglamorous parts that make AI trustworthy
- 🛠️ Most comfortable owning systems end-to-end: from performance profiling to setting up robust observability and catching production edge cases.
